Hi, I'm Crash!

I'm Crash, also known as @this_yeen_got_memes & @crashdayeen on social media! I'm an adventurous spirit who loves exploring the world around me, especially through urbex (urban exploration). You'll often find me climbing art installations, wandering through abandoned places, or just soaking up the energy of new and exciting locations.

Beyond my love for adventure, I'm also passionate about fursuiting and the furry community. I share my fursuit adventures and daily life on Twitter and Instagram, where you can catch glimpses of my quirky personality and creative pursuits. Whether I'm attending conventions, meeting up with friends, or just goofing off in my fursuit, I'm always having a blast and making new memories!

When I'm not exploring or fursuiting, you can probably find me working hard. Life is an adventure, and I'm here to live it to the fullest!
